==================
ONTOLOGY® WORKSPACE
==================

ONTOLOGY™ stores all input and output files in the workspace on your computer. Do not edit the workspace except where noted below, because this could cause unexpected behaviour for ONTOLOGY™

The ONTOLOGY™ workspace is organized by Batch at the top level, then Runs are subfolders within the Batch folder.

The metadata and photos submitted by the user are stored in named folders.
